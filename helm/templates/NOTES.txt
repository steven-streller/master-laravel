Thank you for installing the master-laravel Helm chart!

To access your Laravel application:

By default, the Nginx service is of type ClusterIP and not exposed outside the cluster.
To access your application, use port-forwarding:

  export POD_NAME=$(kubectl get pods --namespace {{ .Release.Namespace }} -l "app={{ include "master-laravel.name" . }},component=nginx" -o jsonpath="{.items[0].metadata.name}")
  kubectl --namespace {{ .Release.Namespace }} port-forward $POD_NAME 8080:80
  echo "Visit http://127.0.0.1:8080"

Health endpoints:
  /livez   - Liveness probe
  /readyz  - Readiness probe
  /healthz - Combined health check
